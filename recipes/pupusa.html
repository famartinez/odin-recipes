Pupusa

Description

Made from masa dough, the base for tortillas, tamales and other Latin cooking staples, pupusas are thick corn cakes filled with a variety of ingredients such as pork, chicken, cheese or beans. For certain, they are always served with curtido, a wonderfully crunchy and tangy cabbage slaw, and a simple red salsa.

Ingredients

  • instant corn masa flour
  • grated queso Oaxaca
  • Curtido
  • Salsa Roja

Steps

  1. Make the Dough
  2. Fill the Pupusas with Cheese
  3. Cook the Pupusas
